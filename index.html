Facts in Nutrition

Explore Important Nutrition Facts


Fact Description
Vitamins Vitamins are essential organic compounds that are crucial for various physiological functions in the body.
Proteins Proteins are macronutrients made up of amino acids, which are the building blocks of tissues and organs.
Carbohydrates Carbohydrates are the body's primary source of energy, found in foods like bread, pasta, and rice.
Fats Fats are essential for energy storage, insulation, and the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins in the body.
Fiber Dietary fiber is important for digestive health, regulating bowel movements, and preventing constipation.
Minerals Minerals such as calcium, iron, and potassium are vital for bone health, oxygen transport, and fluid balance.
Water Water is essential for hydration, regulating body temperature, and facilitating various metabolic processes.
Antioxidants Antioxidants help neutralize free radicals in the body, reducing oxidative stress and lowering the risk of chronic diseases.
Calories Calories are a measure of energy derived from food and beverages, required for bodily functions and physical activity.
Portion Control Controlling portion sizes is essential for weight management and ensuring a balanced intake of nutrients.
